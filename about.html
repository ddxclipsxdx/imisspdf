How It Works

Get started in just a few simple steps.

1

Upload Your PDF

Click "Upload PDF" to select your file. You can also drag and drop multiple files for batch processing.

2

Choose Action

Select your desired action — Convert, Merge, Split, or Compress.

3

Processing

Wait for processing (usually a few seconds). Our servers handle everything securely.

4

Download Result

Click "Download Result" to save your new file. Processed files are automatically deleted after download.

Tip: You can drag and drop multiple files for batch processing.
